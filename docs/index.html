CivicSuite / CivicContracts

Contract records that stay reviewable.

CivicContracts v0.1.1 ships a foundation for contract registry support, clause topic lookup, expiration tracking, renewal visibility, public-records export checklists, and an accessible sample UI aligned to civiccore==0.3.0.

Shipping v0.1.1

What ships

  • Sample contract registry.
  • Clause topic lookup.
  • Expiration tracking helper.
  • Renewal visibility helper.
  • Public-records export checklist.
  • Public sample UI at /civiccontracts.

What does not ship

CivicContracts does not interpret legal obligations, approve renewals, execute contracts, provide legal advice, call live LLMs, use live contract management platforms, or replace a contract system of record.

Repository

github.com/CivicSuite/civiccontracts

Dependency

Pinned to civiccore==0.3.0. CivicCore remains dependency-only; it never imports from CivicContracts.
